Explore Bali

Discover Bali's Most Stunning Destinations

From sacred temples nestled in misty highlands to pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters — let JiE take you on an unforgettable journey across the Island of the Gods.

Tabanan

Tanah Lot Temple

One of Bali's most iconic sea temples, perched on a dramatic rock formation with breathtaking sunset views over the Indian Ocean.

Ubud

Tegallalang Rice Terrace

Walk through stunning emerald-green terraced rice paddies in the cultural heart of Bali. A UNESCO-recognized subak irrigation system.

Badung

Uluwatu Temple

A majestic cliff-top temple 70 meters above the sea. Enjoy the legendary Kecak fire dance performance at sunset.

Bangli

Kintamani & Mount Batur

Marvel at the panoramic view of an active volcano and its caldera lake. Opt for a sunrise trek to the summit for an unforgettable experience.

Klungkung

Nusa Penida Island

Home to the world-famous Kelingking Beach, Angel's Billabong, and Broken Beach. A paradise for adventurers and ocean lovers.

Karangasem

Lempuyang — Gates of Heaven

The iconic split gate framing Mount Agung is Bali's most Instagrammable spot. An ancient temple complex with spiritual significance.

Karangasem

Besakih — Mother Temple

Bali's largest and holiest Hindu temple complex on the slopes of Mount Agung. Over 80 temples with centuries of spiritual heritage.

Gianyar

Tirta Empul Holy Spring

Experience a sacred Balinese purification ritual at this thousand-year-old water temple with crystal-clear natural spring pools.

Badung

Seminyak Beach

Bali's trendiest coastal area with vibrant beach clubs, world-class dining, boutique shopping, and spectacular golden sunsets.
